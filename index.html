FAQ

Common Questions

Have more questions? Call or WhatsApp us anytime.

Ask on WhatsApp
Do I need to book an appointment in advance? +
While walk-ins are always welcome, we recommend booking an appointment in advance — especially for bridal, pre-bridal and party makeup services — to ensure your preferred time slot is available.
What are your working hours? +
We are open all days of the week — Monday through Sunday — from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. No day off, so you can visit us any time that's convenient for you.
Do you offer home services / at-home bridal makeup? +
Yes! We do offer home visits for bridal and occasion makeup within Greater Noida. Please call or WhatsApp us with your location and date to check availability and charges.
What products do you use? +
We use only professional, salon-grade and dermatologist-trusted brands including MAC, Kérastase, Schwarzkopf, L'Oréal Professionnel, Rica, and more. Product safety and quality are our top priorities.
How early should I book my bridal appointment? +
We recommend booking bridal and pre-bridal packages at least 2–4 weeks in advance, especially during wedding seasons (Oct–Feb and April–June) as slots fill up quickly.
Do you offer trial makeup sessions? +
Absolutely! We offer bridal trial sessions where you can preview your makeup look before the wedding day. This helps us understand your preferences and finalize the perfect look for your big day.
